# Troubleshooting

Quick fixes for the issues people hit most. If yours is missing, email [alberto@lid.pro](mailto:alberto@lid.pro).

## I shared my link and the sale didn't count

The buyer has to open your link before they buy. If they reached the product some other way, it counts as a direct sale and there is no commission to pay you.

One common cause: some apps open links in their own built-in browser, and that can break the connection between your link and the sale. Ask your buyer to open the link in a normal browser like Safari or Chrome, then buy.

Commissions cannot be added to a sale after it happens, so share the link itself, everywhere.

## My money isn't showing

Check your balance page first. Payouts land within seconds of a sale.

If the sale was a service, or the product has a refund window, part of the money may be held until the buyer confirms delivery or the window closes. That is normal, and it releases automatically.

If it has been longer than a few minutes and nothing shows, contact us and we will look right away.

## I can't log in

Lid signs you in with your email. Request a login code, then check your inbox and your spam folder. Make sure you are using the same email you signed up with. Still stuck? Email us from that address.

## My buyer wants a refund

If you set a refund window on the product, the buyer can request their money back inside that window and the full amount returns to them, including any commission from the sale. After the window closes, the sale is final. If you set no window, sales are final right away.

If something needs a human decision, email us and we will help.
