# Bounties

A bounty is a challenge with a prize. You post it, people compete, you pick the winners, and everyone is paid instantly.

## Steps

1. Go to **My bounties → New bounty** in your [dashboard](https://lid.pro/dashboard).
2. Describe the challenge and how you will judge it, in plain language.
3. Set the prize and how it splits across winners.
4. Set a deadline, or leave it open.
5. Fund it and publish.

<!-- screenshot: new bounty form with prize and deadline fields -->

The prize money is set aside safely the moment you publish. Participants can see it is there before they start working, so they trust the bounty.

## Picking winners

Submissions arrive in your dashboard. You choose the winners, and each one is paid in seconds.
