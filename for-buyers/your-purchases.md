# Your purchases

Everything you buy on Lid lives in your account. This page shows you where.

## Finding your files

1. Go to [lid.pro](https://lid.pro) and sign in with your email.
2. Open **My purchases** in your dashboard.
3. Select the purchase. Your files are there, ready to download.

<!-- screenshot: the My purchases page with a purchased ebook selected -->

You can download your files as many times as you like.

## Opening your course

1. Sign in at [lid.pro](https://lid.pro).
2. Open **My purchases**.
3. Select the course to open it.

<!-- screenshot: a purchased course opened from My purchases -->

## Getting back in later

Your purchases stay in your account. To get back to them:

1. Go to [lid.pro](https://lid.pro).
2. Sign in with the email you used when you bought.
3. Open **My purchases**.

{% hint style="info" %}
Use the same email every time. Your email is the key to your account.
{% endhint %}

## Common questions

<details><summary>Where did my purchase go?</summary>

Sign in with the exact email you used at checkout, then open **My purchases**. If it is still missing, write to alberto@lid.pro and include the email you paid with.

</details>

<details><summary>Do purchases expire?</summary>

Your purchases stay in your account. Come back next month or next year and they will be there.

</details>

<details><summary>Can I use a new device?</summary>

Yes. Sign in with the same email from any phone, tablet, or computer.

</details>
