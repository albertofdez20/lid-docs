# Your product page

Every product on Lid gets a public page at a URL like:

```
https://lid.pro/p/your-product
```

This page is your product's home. It's where buyers land, where affiliates explore before they share, and where the checkout flow starts.

## What the page shows

* Product title
* Description you wrote
* Price
* Buy button
* "Earn on this product" option (if affiliate commission is greater than 0)
* Trust signals: instant payout, on-chain settlement, 3% infrastructure fee

## The affiliate side of the page

If you enabled affiliate commission, the page has a second surface. Any visitor can click **Earn on this product** and generate their personal share link in one step.

They don't need your permission. They don't sign a contract. They accept the commission you already set. The smart contract does the rest when their link produces a sale.

This is the thing that's different. The product page isn't only a place to buy. It's a place to enroll as a distributor.

## What you can customize today

* Title, description, price
* Delivery file or access link
* Affiliate commission percentage
* Product image or thumbnail
* Slug (the end of the URL)

## What's coming on Pro

Pro tier comes when features justify it. The lead Pro feature is creator-to-buyer communication: email and community tools that close the gap between you and the people who buy from you. Analytics, custom branding, advanced attribution, and priority support sit alongside it. Pro lands at around $29 per month with the 3% transaction fee unchanged.

## What's on the roadmap

## Page performance

The product page is also the front door for attribution, trust, and conversion. Three things matter more than everything else.

1. **Clarity in the first scroll.** What. Who. Outcome.
2. **Price visible above the fold.** No hunting.
3. **Instant payout mentioned.** This is your differentiator. Say so.
