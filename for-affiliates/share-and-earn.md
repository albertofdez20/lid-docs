# Share and earn

Once you have your affiliate link, the next step is simple. Put it where the right people will see it.

## Generate your link

On any product page with affiliate commission enabled, click **Earn on this product**. Your affiliate link is created instantly and looks like:

```
https://app.lid.pro/p/product-slug?ref=your-id
```

That `ref` parameter is what the smart contract reads at checkout to bind attribution to you.

## Where to share

The link works anywhere a URL works. The question is where your audience pays attention.

**High-trust, high-intent channels:**

- Your own newsletter
- Your community (Discord, Telegram, Circle, Slack)
- Direct replies to people who asked for a recommendation
- A pinned tweet or post

**Medium-trust channels:**

- Organic social posts (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Threads, TikTok)
- Reply guy mode on related conversations
- Your link in bio

**Low-trust channels (use carefully):**

- Cold DMs
- Mass outreach
- Forum posts where you're not a regular

The further from the center of your existing audience you go, the harder the conversion. Stay close to home first.

## How to actually sell through a link

Most affiliates post a link with no context and wonder why nothing converts. The link is the last step, not the whole thing.

**Lead with a specific outcome.** "I used this course to ship my first Solana program in a weekend" beats "Check out this course".

**Show the evidence.** Screenshot the thing, show the result, share the receipt.

**Name the audience.** "If you're a frontend dev looking to cross over into Web3, this is the one I'd send you."

**Put the link at the end.** People read the story first. The link is the obvious next step, not the lede.

## What not to do

- **Don't spam.** You'll burn your audience faster than the commission will pay.
- **Don't fake reviews.** Attribution is on-chain. Gaming it catches up.
- **Don't hide that you're earning.** Disclosure builds trust. "Full disclosure: I earn a commission if you buy" is a three-second line that protects the relationship.
- **Don't promote products you haven't touched.** Your audience can tell the difference.

## Tracking your performance

Your dashboard at [app.lid.pro](https://app.lid.pro) shows:

- Clicks on each affiliate link
- Conversions
- Commission earned (total and per product)
- Payout history (every split you've received)

When Pro launches, analytics will deepen. Per-channel attribution, conversion funnels, time-to-purchase, repeat buyer tracking.

## Earnings cadence

Every sale attributed to you pays out the commission immediately, in the same transaction as the sale. There is no weekly payout. No minimum balance. No withdrawal request.

Your account balance grows as sales close.
