# Is Lid safe?

Short version: nobody at Lid can touch your money, because Lid never holds it. Here is how that works.

## Who holds the money

Nobody. When a buyer pays, the payment splits and lands in each person's account the second it happens. The seller gets their share. The person who shared the link gets theirs. Lid gets a flat 3%. There is no waiting period and no payout day. Your balance is yours the moment you earn it.

## Why the split can't be changed later

The split rules are locked in when a product is created, and everyone can see them before they share or buy. Once a sale happens, the record of that sale is permanent. Nobody can go back and change who got paid, including Lid.

## What protects buyers

Sellers can set a refund window on their products. During that window, the money is held to one side and the buyer can ask for it back. For services, the buyer confirms the work was delivered before the money is released. Payments become final when both sides are done.

## Is this crypto?

Lid runs on blockchain technology, which is what lets payments split and settle in seconds. You never have to touch, understand, or own any of it. You sign up with your email, you see prices in dollars, and you get paid in digital dollars. One digital dollar is worth one US dollar.

## What is still being built

Card payments and cashing out to a bank account are coming. Memberships are coming. The technology behind Lid is reviewed continuously, and an independent external security review is planned.
