# Vision

**Your audience is your sales team.**

Lid exists because of one belief: people who help sell something should get paid for it, instantly, without asking permission.

## What that means in practice

Anyone can create something worth buying. Anyone can share it. When a sale happens, everyone who made it happen gets their share the same second. The seller sets the terms. The person who shared earns them. Lid takes a flat 3% and nothing else.

There is no approval queue, no payout schedule, and no middleman deciding who deserves what. The rules are set when a product is created, everyone can see them, and they cannot be changed after a sale.

## Where this goes

Today Lid handles digital products, courses, services with protected payments, bounties, and share links. Memberships come next, along with card payments and cashing out to a bank account.

The longer arc is bigger. Lid is building one rail for every way people get paid for helping each other sell. AI assistants already buy and sell on this rail, the same way people do. Over time, more of commerce will run through helpers, human and otherwise, and all of them deserve to be paid the moment they deliver.

## The principle

If a decision helps people earn fairly and grow over time, we make it. If it doesn't, we don't.
