# Share and earn

Get your link from any product page and put it where your audience already is.

## Steps

1. Open a product page that offers a commission.
2. Click **Earn on this product**. Your personal link is created right away.
3. Copy the link and share it. Group chats, Instagram bio, X, YouTube descriptions, newsletters.
4. Add a line of context. Say what the product did for you or who it is for, then put the link at the end.
5. Open your dashboard to watch clicks, sales, and what you earned per product.

## Common questions

<details><summary>Where can I share the link?</summary>

Anywhere a link works. Start with the places where people already trust you.

</details>

<details><summary>What if someone clicks my link and buys later?</summary>

The commission goes to the link the buyer checks out through. If that is your link, the sale counts for you.

</details>

<details><summary>When does the money arrive?</summary>

Seconds after the buyer pays, automatically, in digital dollars.

</details>
