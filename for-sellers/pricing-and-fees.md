# Pricing and fees

You set the price. Lid takes a flat 3% on every sale, shown up front. That is the whole model.

## The three numbers

* **Your price.** You choose it and you can change it anytime.
* **Lid's fee.** A flat 3% of every sale.
* **The commission you share.** From 1% to 50%, paid to whoever brings you a sale. You choose it per product.

## A $10 sale, worked out

Say you set a 10% commission and someone's shared link brings the sale:

| Who        | Gets  |
| ---------- | ----- |
| You        | $8.70 |
| The sharer | $1.00 |
| Lid        | $0.30 |

If you bring the sale yourself, you keep $9.70 and Lid gets $0.30.

## Why 3%

Most platforms take 5% to 30% once subscriptions and processing costs stack up. Lid charges one flat fee that covers everything: your page, checkout, delivery, and paying everyone their share in seconds. Nothing hidden, and the 3% stays put as you grow.

## What you never pay

* Monthly fees
* Listing fees
* Withdrawal fees
* Minimums of any kind
