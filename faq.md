## Selling

<details><summary>What can I sell on Lid?</summary>

Digital products, courses, services with protected payments, and bounties are live today. Memberships are coming.

</details>

<details><summary>What does Lid cost?</summary>

A flat 3% of each sale. There is no monthly fee and no setup cost.

</details>

<details><summary>How fast do I get paid?</summary>

Within seconds of the sale. The payment splits automatically the moment it happens, and your share lands in your balance.

</details>

<details><summary>Do I need to know anything technical?</summary>

No. You sign up with your email, set your price in dollars, and share your link. Everything else happens automatically.

</details>

<details><summary>Can other people help me sell?</summary>

Yes. Turn on commission for your product and anyone can grab a share link. You choose the commission, from 1% to 50% of each sale.

</details>

<details><summary>Can I change the commission later?</summary>

Yes, going forward. Sales that already happened keep the split they were made under.

</details>

<details><summary>Can buyers get refunds?</summary>

You choose a refund window when you create a product. Inside that window a buyer can ask for their money back. If you set no window, you are paid instantly and sales are final.

</details>

<details><summary>Can I sell memberships or subscriptions?</summary>

Coming. Today Lid handles one-time purchases.

</details>

<details><summary>Do I own my customer relationships?</summary>

Yes. Your buyers, your content, and your pricing are yours. Lid is the rail underneath.

</details>

## Sharing and earning

<details><summary>How do I earn by sharing?</summary>

Find a product with commission turned on, grab your share link, and post it anywhere. When someone buys through your link, your share lands in your balance within seconds.

</details>

<details><summary>How much can I earn per sale?</summary>

The seller sets the commission, anywhere from 1% to 50% of the price. You can see it before you share.

</details>

<details><summary>Do I need approval to share a product?</summary>

No. If commission is on, anyone can share and earn. There is no application and no waiting.

</details>

<details><summary>When do I get my commission?</summary>

The moment the sale happens. There is no payout day and no minimum balance.

</details>

<details><summary>What happens if the buyer gets a refund?</summary>

The whole payment returns to the buyer, including your commission. Refunds undo the full sale for everyone.

</details>

## Buying

<details><summary>What do I need to buy something?</summary>

An email address. Checkout works like any online purchase, and your purchases stay in your account.

</details>

<details><summary>How do I pay?</summary>

With digital dollars, each worth one US dollar. Card payments are coming.

</details>

<details><summary>Can I get a refund?</summary>

If the seller set a refund window, you can request your money back inside it and the full amount returns to you.

</details>

<details><summary>Is my payment protected?</summary>

Your payment goes straight to the right people the second it happens, and nobody can change that afterward. For services, the money is held until you confirm the work was delivered.

</details>

<details><summary>Where do I find what I bought?</summary>

Log in at [lid.pro](https://lid.pro) with the email you used at checkout. Your purchases are in your account.

</details>
