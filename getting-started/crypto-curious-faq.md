# Crypto-curious FAQ

For creators and affiliates who don't think of themselves as crypto people. Lid is built for you.

## Do I need to understand blockchain to use Lid?

No. Lid is infrastructure. The blockchain runs in the background. You see a dashboard, a product page, a payout. You don't see chains, gas, or wallet addresses unless you go looking for them.

## Will my buyers need a wallet?

No. A buyer lands on a checkout. They pay. The wallet is created for them invisibly through Privy. They never know it exists.

## What's a "digital dollar"?

A USDC stablecoin. One USDC equals one US dollar. The price stays at $1.00. Lid uses digital dollars because they settle in under a second and the value is predictable.

## What if the digital dollar loses its 1:1 value (a "depeg")?

USDC is issued by Circle and backed by reserves held at regulated US banks. Depegs in USDC's history have been brief and resolved. If something material changes, Lid will publish a clear update. Today, USDC is one of the most reserve-backed stablecoins in the market.

## Do I have to know what Solana is?

No. Solana is the network underneath. You'll feel its speed (sub-second settlements) and its cost (sub-cent fees). You won't have to think about it.

## Can I get paid in regular dollars?

Not yet inside Lid. You earn digital dollars, instantly, and they are yours: you can send them to any wallet or exchange today. A built-in cash-out to your bank account is planned and will land after the raise. We will not claim it before it ships.

## What if I lose my password?

Account recovery works the way you expect. Email reset. No seed phrase to write down. Privy handles the wallet recovery in the background.

## What if Lid disappears?

The smart contract is on Solana. Your past sales, attribution records, and ownership records stay on-chain regardless of what happens to Lid as a company. The records belong to you, not to a database.

## Is this regulated?

Lid is non-custodial. Funds settle directly between parties through a smart contract. Lid never holds your money. Each region's regulatory treatment of stablecoins differs. We watch the rules and stay on the right side of them.

## Is this safer than a regular platform?

Safer where it matters most: payouts and attribution. The smart contract can't be edited after a sale to change who gets paid. A spreadsheet at a traditional platform can.

## What's still being earned?

External smart contract audit. Repeatability across hundreds of loops. Phase 2 self-serve alpha. We're honest about what's done and what isn't. The [Status page](../status.md) shows current numbers.
